Catering Services

Thank you for choosing DT Parfaits! We are proud to offer a variety of catering services to fit the needs of your occasion or event.

  • Pick Up - At no additional charge we will have your orders ready for pick up at our location. Food and disposables will be presented to you to take to your event. Additional charge to include plates, cups, or cutlery.

  • Drop Off Delivery - 10% additional charge for every 5 miles from DT Parfaits location.

Born and raised in Texas, Dalena Tran grew up in Friendswood, TX attending Clear Brook High School class of 2024. With a large family who are all foodies, sparked her lifelong passion for food and to provide for family gatherings with sweet delicacies. Her natural love for food and her family-oriented personality ultimately called her to the catering business. After graduating, she plans to major in business and with DT Parfaits, she gains hands on experience within the business field and strengthens her passion for desserts.

“There is nothing more fulfilling than finding that special touch and making people happy. When you create a special culinary experience for your clients and their guests, it is truly a magical feeling seeing them stuff their mouths with your creations.”
